"ShingletownShingletown, CAFavoriteShareMoreNearbyDirectionsSponsored TopicsDescriptionShingletown is a census-designated place (CDP) in Shasta County, California, United States. The population was 2,283 at the 2010 census, up from 2,222 at the 2000 census. Shingletown is a small town located along California State Route 44 in the hills just below Mount Lassen. Shingletown has a history of logging large timber including pine, fir and cedar. It is also known as ""The Gateway to Lassen"". Shingletown is located at 40°29′46″N 121°51′49″W / 40.49611°N 121.86361°W / 40.49611; -121.86361 (40.496033, -121.863657) in upstate California, near Lassen Volcanic National Park."
