Depending on the role they shoulder, clinical nurse managers are often less involved in clinical nursing and more involved in management on a clinical level.Clinical nurse managers are usually at pay band 8a, but up to 8b/8c which corresponds to H gradeunder the Whitley system, which was officially retired with the Agenda for Change system but maystill be used in the working parlance.
