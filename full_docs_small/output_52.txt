All Rights Reserved.Gross IncomeAn individual or company's income before taxes and deductions. For individual income, it is calculated as the individual's wages or salary, investment and asset appreciation, and theamount made from any other source of income. In a company, it is calculated as revenuesminus expenses. An individual's gross income is important to determining eligibility forcertain social programs, while a company's gross income is one measure among many of how well it uses its resources to produce a profit.
