Dutch tuit ""sprout, snout,"" Middle Dutch tute ""nipple, pap,"" Middle Low German tute ""horn, funnel,"" Old Norse tota ""teat, toe of a shoe""). The sense developed to ""look out for jobs, votes, etc., to try to get them"" (1731), then ""praise highly"" (1920).
