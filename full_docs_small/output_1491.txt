This is seen is Modern Warfare 2 in the case of the model 1887. EditNerf is also used in many genres or games, and it means the same thing in each one, to make something worse. Always happens with patches/updates. Something that starts off awful, isn't nerfed, and depending on how awful, it will be 'buffed' or made better.
