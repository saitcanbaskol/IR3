"What is diabetes?Diabetes is a group of conditions linked by an inability to produce enough insulin and/or to respond to insulin. This causes high blood glucose levels  ( hyperglycemia) and can lead to a number of acute and chronic health problems, some of them life-threatening.Diabetes is the seventh leading cause of death in the United States.
