"Des PlainesDes Plaines, ILFavoriteShareMoreNearbyDirectionsSponsored TopicsDescriptionDes Plaines ( /dɛspleɪnz/) is a city in Cook County, Illinois, United States. It has adopted the official nickname of ""City of Destiny."" As of the 2000 census, the city had a total population of 58,720. It is a suburb of Chicago, and is next to O'Hare International Airport. The Des Plaines River runs through the city, just east of its downtown area. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 14.5 square miles (37.6 km²).Of this, 14.4 square miles (37.3 km²) of it is land and 0.1 square miles (0.3 km²) of it (0.76%) is water. Des Plaines is traversed by two interstate highways – I-90 Northwest Tollway and I-294 Tri-State Tollway. Des Plaines is named for the Des Plaines River, which flows through it. The name is French, ""of the plains."""
