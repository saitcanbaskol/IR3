"mondegreenAlso found in: Wikipedia .mon·de·green (mŏn′də-grēn′, môn′-)n.A series of words that result from the mishearing or misinterpretation of a statement or songlyric. For example, I led the pigeons to the flag for I pledge allegiance to the flag. [After  (Lady) Mondegreen, a misinterpretation of the line  (hae laid) him on the green, from the song ""The Bonny Earl of Murray"" .]American Heritage® Dictionary of the English Language, Fifth Edition.
