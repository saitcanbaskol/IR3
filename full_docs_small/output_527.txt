He serves as the cook of Castle Black .Season 3On their way back to the Wall from the mutiny at Craster's Keep , Sam mentions to Gilly that Hobb makes a very tasty venison stew with onions.  [1]Season 4Hobb prepares to butcher wildlings with his hog-splitter.While staying at Castle Black, Gilly plucks geese and takes them to Hobb in the kitchens.  [2]Acting Commander Thorne warns Jon Snow that Hobb will serve his direwolf Ghost for supper if the creature isn't locked up.  [3]During the Battle of Castle Black, a small wildling party comes from the south and breaches the walls of the keep, and some of them break into the kitchen. Hobb calmly walks up to the wildlings, still wearing his apron, and lands his hog-splitter into the chest of one of them.
