"Nouakchott Climate & TemperatureNouakchott, Mauritania is at 18°6'N, 15°57'W, 2 m (7 ft). See map .Nouakchott has a subtropical desert / low-latitude arid hot climate (Köppen-Geiger classification: BWh)According to the Holdridge life zones system of bioclimatic classification Nouakchott is situated in or near the tropical desert biome.The mean annual temperature is 25.8 degrees Celsius (78.4 degrees Fahrenheit). See the temperatures page for a monthly breakdown and the fixed scale graph.Average monthly temperatures vary by 8 °C (14.4°F). This indicates that the continentality type is hyperoceanic, subtype barely hyperoceanic.Total annual Precipitation averages 157 mm (6.2 inches) which is equivalent to 157 Litres/m² (3.85 Gallons/ft²).On average there are 3251 hours of sunshine per year. Visit the sunshine and daylight section to check monthly details including how high in the sky the sun reaches each month.Were you to burrow down through the centre of the Earth from Nouakchott you would pop up nearest to the climate station at Koumac, New Caledonia."
